Space shooter tutorial

In this tutorial we will program the gamespace shooter.

The game works so that the background moves indefinitely.
The sapce ship (player) must avoid the meteors to protect the Earth.

*video contains audio

The player controls the knight using the A keys (moves left), D (moves right) and the space bar (shoots). The game is gradually accelerating.
You can turn the music on or off.
